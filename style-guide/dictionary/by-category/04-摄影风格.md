# 摄影风格 Photography Styles

本章收录了主要的摄影风格，从传统摄影到数字摄影，从技术分类到艺术风格。

This chapter covers major photography styles, from traditional to digital, from technical categories to artistic styles.

---

## 按题材分类 By Subject

### 人像摄影 Portrait Photography

**简介**：
以人物为主体的摄影，强调人物的表情、性格和情感。

**子风格 Sub-styles**：

| 风格 Style | 特征 Features | 关键词 Keywords |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| 棚拍人像 Studio Portrait | 可控光线、干净背景 | `studio portrait`, `professional headshot` |
| 环境人像 Environmental | 人物与环境结合 | `environmental portrait`, `lifestyle` |
| 街头人像 Street Portrait | 自然、随机 | `street portrait`, `candid` |
| 艺术人像 Fine Art Portrait | 艺术性、概念性 | `fine art portrait`, `artistic` |

**Prompt 关键词**：
`portrait photography`, `headshot`, `portrait`, `face`, `expression`, `professional portrait`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
professional portrait photography, young woman,
soft studio lighting, clean background,
sharp focus on eyes, natural expression,
high-end fashion magazine quality
```

---

### 风景摄影 Landscape Photography

**简介**：
以自然或城市景观为主体的摄影，强调场景的壮丽和氛围。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`landscape photography`, `nature photography`, `scenic`, `panoramic`, `vista`, `golden hour landscape`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
epic landscape photography, mountain range at sunset,
golden hour light, dramatic clouds,
foreground wildflowers, deep depth of field,
National Geographic quality
```

---

### 街头摄影 Street Photography

**简介**：
在公共场所捕捉日常生活瞬间的摄影风格，强调真实性和决定性瞬间。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`street photography`, `candid`, `urban`, `documentary`, `decisive moment`, `Henri Cartier-Bresson style`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
street photography, busy city intersection,
candid moment, people in motion,
black and white, high contrast,
documentary style, decisive moment
```

---

### 静物摄影 Still Life Photography

**简介**：
以静态物体为主体的摄影，常用于产品、食物和艺术创作。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`still life photography`, `product photography`, `tabletop`, `arranged objects`, `commercial`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
still life photography, vintage objects arrangement,
soft window light, wooden table surface,
Dutch masters inspired, moody atmosphere,
artistic composition
```

---

### 建筑摄影 Architectural Photography

**简介**：
以建筑物为主体的摄影，强调建筑的线条、形式和空间感。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`architectural photography`, `building`, `interior photography`, `exterior`, `geometric`, `symmetrical`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
architectural photography, modern skyscraper,
dramatic upward angle, geometric patterns,
blue hour lighting, glass and steel,
clean lines, professional quality
```

---

### 美食摄影 Food Photography

**简介**：
以食物为主体的摄影，强调食物的美味和吸引力。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`food photography`, `culinary`, `appetizing`, `restaurant quality`, `overhead shot`, `45 degree angle`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
food photography, gosta dish,
45 degree angle, soft natural light,
shallow depth of field, fresh ingredients visible,
restaurant menu quality, appetizing
```

## 按技术分类 By Technique

### 黑白摄影 Black and White

**简介**：
去除色彩，专注于光影、纹理和构图的摄影风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`black and white photography`, `monochrome`, `B&W`, `grayscale`, `high contrast B&W`, `film noir`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
black and white portrait, dramatic lighting,
high contrast, deep shadows, film grain,
classic Hollywood glamour, timeless elegance
```

---

### 胶片摄影 Film Photography

**简介**：
使用胶片拍摄或模拟胶片效
**常见胶片 Common Films**：

| 胶片 Film | 特征 Features | 关键词 Keywords |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Kodak Portra | 温暖肤色、柔和 | `Kodak Portra 400`, `warm tones` |
| Fuji Velvia | 高饱和、鲜艳 | `Fuji Velvia`, `vivid colors` |
| Kodak Ektar | 细腻、鲜艳 | `Kodak Ektar 100`, `fine grain` |
| Ilford HP5 | 黑白、颗粒感 | `Ilford HP5`, `grainy B&W` |
| Cinestill 800T | 电影感、霓虹晕染 | `Cinestill 800T`, `halation` |

**Prompt 关键词**：
`film photography`, `analog`, `35mm film`, `film grain`, `shot on film`, `vintage film look`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
shot on Kodak P 400, portrait,
warm skin tones, soft film grain,
natural light, analog photography aesthetic,
nostalgic and timeless
```

---

### 长曝光 Long Exposure

**简介**：
使用长时间曝光捕捉运动轨迹或平滑效果的技术。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`long exposure`, `light trails`, `smooth water`, `motion blur`, `night photography`, `silky water`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
long exposure photography, waterfall,
silky smooth water effect, 30 second exposure,
misty atmosphere, lush green forest,
tripod shot, professional landscape
```

---

### 微距摄影 Macro Photography

**简介**：
极近距离拍摄小物体，展现肉眼难以看到的细节。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`macro photography`, `close-up`, `extreme detail`, `shallow depth of field`, `magnified`

**示例 Prompt**：
```hotography, dewdrop on flower petal,
extreme close-up, shallow depth of field,
morning light, crystal clear water droplet,
intricate details visible
```

---

### 航拍摄影 Aerial/Drone Photography

**简介**：
从高空俯拍的摄影视角。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`aerial photography`, `drone shot`, `bird's eye view`, `top-down`, `overhead view`, `DJI`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
aerial drone photography, winding river through forest,
bird's eye view, autumn colors,
abstract patterns, nature from above,
DJI quality, sharp details
```

---

### 双重曝光 Double Exposure

**简介**：
将两张或多张图像叠加在一起的技术。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`double exposure`, `multiple exposure`, `overlay`, `blended images`, `surreal portrait`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
double exposure portrait, woman's profile,
forest trees filling the silhouette,
dreamy and artistic, nature and human merged,
creative photography technique
```

## 按风格分类 By Style

### 电影感摄影 Cinematic Photography

**简介**：
模拟电影画面质感的摄影风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`cinematic`, `movie still`, `film look`, `anamorphic`, `cinematic lighting`, `widescreen`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
cinematic photography, noir detective scene,
dramatic side lighting, smoke atmosphere,
anamorphic lens flare, 2.35:1 aspect ratio,
movie still quality, moody and atmospheric
```

---

### 高调摄影 High Key

**简介**：
整体明亮、阴影极少的摄影风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`high key`, `bright`, `white background`, `minimal shadows`, `clean`, `airy`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
high key portrait photography, bright white background,
minimal shadows, clean and fresh look,
fashion editorial style, luminous skin
```

---

### 低调摄影 Low Key

**简介**：
整体较暗、大面积阴影的摄影风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`low key`, `dark`, `dramatic shadows`, `moody`, `chiaroscuro`, `noir`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
low key portrait, face emerging from darkness,
single light source, deep dramatic shadows,
film noir style, mysterious atmosphere
```

---

### 复古/怀旧 Vintage/Nostalgic

**简介**：
模拟过去时代摄影效果的风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`vintage photography`, `retro`, `nostalgic`, `faded colors`, `old photo`, `aged`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
vintage 1970s photography style, family picnic,
faded warm colors, soft focus edges,
film grain, light leaks, nostalgic summer day
```

---

### 极简摄影 Minimalist Photography

**简介**：
简化元素、强调留白和简洁构图的摄影风格。

**Prompt 关键词**：
`minimalist photography`, `simple`, `negative space`, `clean compoion`, `less is more`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
minimalist photography, single tree on horizon,
vast empty sky, simple composition,
lots of negative space, zen aesthetic,
peaceful and contemplative
```
