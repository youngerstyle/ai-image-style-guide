# 清新自然 Fresh and Natural Styles

当你想要创造清新、自然、治愈系的图像时，可以参考以下风格。

When you want to create fresh, natural, healing images, refer to these styles.

---

## 艺术风格 Art Styles

### 水彩风格 Watercolor
**关键词**：`watercolor`, `soft washes`, `transparent`, `delicate`, `flowing`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
watercolor illustration, spring garden,
soft transparent washes, delicate flowers,
light and airy, gentle colors,
artistic and fresh
```

### 印象派 Impressionism
**关键词**：`impressionist`, `light and color`, `outdoor`, `natural light`, `Monet style`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
impressionist painting, flower garden,
dappled sunlight, visible brushstrokes,
Monet inspired, peaceful atmosphere,
vibrant natural colors
```

### 自然主义 Naturalism
**关键词**：`naturalistic`, `realistic nature`, `true to life`, `natural beauty`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
naturalistic landscape painting,
meadow with wildflowers, realistic detail,
golden hour light, serene atmosphere,
celebrating natural beauty
```

**关键词**：`natural light`, `golden hour`, `soft light`, `outdoor photography`

**示例 Prompt**：
```

```

### 风景摄影 Landscape Photography
**关键词**：`landscape photography`, `nature`, `scenic`, `wilderness`, `pristine`

**示例 Prompt**：
```

fresh morning air, untouched nature,
National Geographic quality
```

### 植物摄影 Botanical Photography
**关键词**：`botanical photography`, `plant photography`, `macro flowers`, `garden`

**示例 Prompt**：
```

soft natural light, shallow depth of field,

```
