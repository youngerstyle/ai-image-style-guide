# 优雅精致 Elegant and Refined Styles

当你想要创造高级、优雅、精致的图像时，可以参考以下风格。

When you want to create sophisticated, elegant, refined images, refer to these styles.

---

## 艺术风格 Art Styles

### 新艺术运动 Art Nouveau
**关键词**：`Art Nouveau`, `Mucha style`, `flowing lines`, `organic curves`, `decorative`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
Art Nouveau illustration, elegant woman,
flowing organic lines, floral decorative borders,
Alphonse Mucha inspired, muted earth tones,
ornate and sophisticated
```

### 装饰艺术 Art Deco
**关键词**：`Art Deco`, `geometric`, `gold and black`, `luxurious`, `1920s glamour`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
Art Deco design, geometric patterns,
gold and black color scheme, symmetrical,
luxurious and sophisticated, Gatsby era elegance
```

### 古典主义 Classical
**关键词**：`classical`, `Renaissance`, `Baroque`, `old master`, `museum quality`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
classical oil painting style, elegant portrait,
Renaissance technique, rich colors,
dramatic lighting, museum masterpiece quality
```

---

## 设计风格 Design Styles

### 极简主义 Minimalism
**关键词**：`minimalist`, `minimal`, `clean`, `simple`, `less is more`, `white space`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
minimalist design, elegant simplicity,
lots of white space, single focal point,
clean lines, sophisticated restraint,
modern and refined
```

### 北欧设计 Scandinavian Design
**关键词**：`Scandinavian`, `Nordic`, `hygge`, `natural materials`, `functional elegance`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
Scandinavian interior design, elegant simplicity,
natural wood and white, soft textures,
warm minimalism, cozy yet sophisticated
```

### 日式美学 Japanese Aesthetics
**关键词**：`Japanese aesthetic`, `wabi-sabi`, `zen`, `minimalist Japanese`, `ikebana`

**示例 Prompt**：
```
Japanese zen aesthetic, elegant simplicity,
single branch ikebana, negative space,
wabi-sabi beauty, serene and refined
```
